The bit you can't revise for

Papers can be drilled. The interview can't.

Your child can sit a hundred papers — but the interview is a conversation with a stranger, and there's rarely a way to rehearse it.

Mock interviews are scarce

A private coach costs £80–£100 an hour and books out months ahead. Most families get one go, if any.

Bright children freeze up

Nerves, one-word answers, going blank on "tell me about yourself." Confidence counts as much as the answer.

Parents can't see the gap

You know something's off but not what to fix — so practice at home goes in circles.
